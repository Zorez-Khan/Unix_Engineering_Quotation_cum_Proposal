Overview
Introduction & Value Proposition

We are pleased to submit this Quotation cum Proposal for onsite manpower management at the Tata Steel TSUISL Golmuri Town Management Depot. Unix Engineering offers an end-to-end workforce deployment model — combining manpower supply, daily supervision, quality oversight, material handling coordination, and muster roll maintenance under a single unified engagement. This eliminates the need for the client to deploy additional supervisory personnel for routine site management functions.

💡
Cost Efficiency for Client: Our bundled service model clubs manpower supply & job completion monitoring with QC, material handling, and muster maintenance — removing the expense of deploying separate client-side personnel for these functions. One engagement covers all.

Pricing
Rate Schedule
Service A — Onsite Manpower Supply & Deployment Management
₹150 / Head / Day
Covers supply, scheduling, and daily deployment of manpower with work allocation to each worker and monitoring of daily job completion. Includes coordination between site engineer requirements and labour availability.
Formula Total = Headcount × ₹150  |  e.g. 6 workers → 6 × ₹150 = ₹900/day
Work Allocation Job Completion Monitoring Daily Deployment Headcount Management
Service B — QC Supervision, Material Handling & Muster Roll Maintenance
₹200 + ₹20/Head/Day
Base charge covers supervision setup, documentation, and administrative overhead. Per-head charge covers quality checking of work output, material movement coordination, and daily muster / attendance record maintenance for each deployed worker.
Formula Total = ₹200 + (Headcount × ₹20)  |  e.g. 6 workers → ₹200 + (6 × ₹20) = ₹320/day
QC Supervision Material Handling Muster Roll Attendance Records
📦
Bundled Advantage: Engaging both Service A and Service B together provides the client with a fully managed onsite workforce — from deployment to quality verification — without the overhead of maintaining a separate internal supervisory team. This directly reduces client-side manpower costs and administrative burden.
